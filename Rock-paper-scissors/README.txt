Rock-Paper-Scissors
This is a simple rock-paper-scissors game that can be played between two human players, or between a human player and a computer player.

How to play
Choose a player name.
Select your move: rock, paper, or scissors.
The computer will randomly select a move.
The winner is determined by the following rules:
Rock beats scissors.
Paper beats rock.
Scissors beats paper.
The game continues until one player has won 3 games.
